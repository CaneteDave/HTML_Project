Nike Air Force 1

The Nike Air Force 1 Low is inarguably one of the most
essential sneakers of all time. First debuting on the
basketball court in 1982, the Air Force 1 quickly
quickly became a staple of the streets where it’s
remained ever since. When sneaker technology progressed
and the Air Force 1 was no longer commonly found on the
court, it was then adopted by the sneaker fanatics and
hip-hop kids in New York City in the mid ’80s. Since then,
it’s gone on to be one of the most worn shoes ever, with
millions of pairs sold worldwide.
